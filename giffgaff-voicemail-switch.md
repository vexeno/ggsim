## 🔇 为什么要关闭语音信箱

漫游的时候太贵了，保号卡用不着这个。

## 🛠️ 如何操作

我们是漫游使用，打电话 `1626` 没有用，必须联系客服专员。

1. 登录官网，点击上方菜单的`help`

   ![giffgaff-help-link.png](https://www.giffgaff.shop/_astro/giffgaff-help-link.Bl85ueqY_1AbAgh.webp)

2. 在里面搜索 `voicemail` ，找到 `Everything you need to know about voicemail`：

   ![giffgaff-help-voicemail-search.png](https://www.giffgaff.shop/_astro/giffgaff-help-voicemail-search.DgOd1us8_Z2hKHGj.webp)

   也可以直接访问链接：[Everything you need to know about voicemail](https://help.giffgaff.com/en/articles/629442-everything-you-need-to-know-about-voicemail)

3. 点击里面 `Manage your voicemail settings` 部分的 agents 链接：

   ![giffgaff-voicemail-agent.png](https://www.giffgaff.shop/_astro/giffgaff-voicemail-agent.BUzl2iV2_2eKh8A.webp)

   也可以直接访问链接：[Get in touch with an agent](https://support2.giffgaff.com/app/ask/International-and-Roaming/Accessing-voicemail-while-abroad/form/)

4. 填写表单：

   ![giffgaff-voicemail-agent-form.png](https://www.giffgaff.shop/_astro/giffgaff-voicemail-agent-form.B395rFU-_NpN5h.webp)

   留言参考话术：

   ```text
   I'm using giffgaff roaming in China, I'd like to turn off my voicemail.
   ```

5. 提交后就会创建工单，等客服处理。后续在官网菜单 `Help` -> `Agent messages` 处进入，查看处理进度，链接地址：[Messages from agents](https://www.giffgaff.com/support/questions) 。
